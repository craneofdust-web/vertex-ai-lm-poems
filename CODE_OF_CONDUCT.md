# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect all participants to:
- be respectful and constructive
- focus on technical issues, not personal attacks
- communicate clearly and in good faith

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, abusive language, and repeated bad-faith disruption.

## Scope

This applies to repository spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and project communication channels.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact maintainers through a private channel.
For security-sensitive issues, follow `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit/remove content, issue warnings, or limit participation when behavior violates this policy.
